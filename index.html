This is a (G)IDLE fanpage!

(G)IDLE, stylized in all caps, is a South Korean girl group formed by Cube Entertainment in 2018. The group consist of five members: Minnie, Miyeon, Soyeon, Yuqi, and Shuhua. Originally a six-piece, Soojin departed from the group on August 14, 2021. The group debuted on May 2, 2018, with the single "Latata" from their first extended play I Am. On July 31, 2019, the group debuted in Japan under U-Cube with Latata.
